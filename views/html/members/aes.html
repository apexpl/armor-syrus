Encrypt / Decrypt Data

Below you may test the AES encryp / decrypt functionality of Armor. Every user automatically has a 4096 bit RSA keypair generated upon creation, and all data encryptede to your account is encrypted to your personal RSA key. This segregates and helps protect the encrypted data, as if an attacker ever manages to access the database, they will not have full access to all encrypted data since it's all encrypted to each individual user's RSA key.
